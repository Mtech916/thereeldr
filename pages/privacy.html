Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Decemeber 5, 2023

1. Information Collected:

We collect email addresses when users voluntarily provide them for contact purposes. We do not collect any other personally identifiable information without your consent.

1.1 Purpose of Collection:

The collected email addresses are used solely for communication purposes. We will not use your email address for any other purpose without obtaining your explicit consent.

2. Data Storage:

Email addresses are securely stored on our email servers. We may use third-party services for email management, and we ensure that they adhere to industry-standard security measures.

2.1 Data Security:

We take the security of your information seriously. We implement measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your email address.

3. Third-party Sharing:

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your email addresses to third parties without your consent. However, we may use third-party services for email communication, and these service providers are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

  • 3.1 Opt-out Option:
    You have the right to opt-out of receiving further communications from us. To do so, please email us and we will delete you info after our communications have ended.

  • 3.2 Legal Compliance:
    We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

  • 3.3 Policy Updates:
    This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The last revision date will be indicated at the top of this page.

About The Reel Dr.

Established in 1991, I started this business after taking my reels to a local fishing store to get serviced, they would work great for a few hours and then I'd experience the same issues. So, I decided I would learn how to do it myself and have been working on reels ever since. Fishing is in my blood!
