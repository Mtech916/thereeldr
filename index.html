Cleaning

Keeping the internal moving parts of your reel clean and lubed ensures longer last parts. That means you spend less and take more time fishing.

So creating an optimal environment for all your components is essential.

Maintenance

Sometimes, cleaning isn't enough and after a cleaning we may find that some but not of your components new replacing or extra attention.

In this case, we are able to replace components due to wear and tear from the elements.

Repairs

Repairs are needs due to lack of cleaning and maintanence. If your last fishing adventure resulted in some damage to the housing or internal components, we have you covered.

Repairing and or replacing broken and damaged components takes time and patience.

Customized Repairs

Even if you've done all you need, cleaning, maintenance, and repairs. Sometimes, we find not all components are available for replacement and that's where we shine.

The Reel Dr. specializes in customized repairs to keep your favorite reel working optimally.
