Meet the Team

I'm Ron, The Reel Dr. - Established in 1991, I started this business after taking my reels to a local fishing store to get serviced, they would work great for a few hours and then I'd experience the same issues. So, I decided I would learn how to do it myself and have been working on reels ever since. Fishing is in my blood!

I'm Matt and I've spent the last ten years as a diesel mechanic and love tinkering and fixing things. Recently, I needed to pivot out of heavy mechanics and partnered with The Reel Dr. to develope this website and fix fishing reels.
